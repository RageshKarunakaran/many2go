Description

  • Marina Bay Sands – Singapore's iconic integrated resort and lifestyle destination! It's not just a hotel – it's a city within a city, offering world-class entertainment, dining, shopping, and accommodation all under one roof. With its striking architecture and breathtaking views of the city skyline, Marina Bay Sands is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking luxury and excitement.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Visit - Mid-afternoon to evening hours

      Things To Do - Experience rooftop infinity pools, visit the observation deck, shop, dine, and watch world class entertainment

      Perfect Fit For - Shopaholics, Architecture Admirers

    • Description

  • Gardens by the Bay, Singapore's iconic horticultural attraction spanning 101 hectares. Explore themed gardens, futuristic Supertrees, and two striking conservatories showcasing diverse flora. Marvel at the innovative fusion of nature and technology in this urban oasis.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - December to June

      Things To Do - Explore themed gardens, walk among towering Supertrees, visit biodomes, and enjoy light shows

    Perfect Fit For - Nature Admirers, Photography Enthusiasts

    • Description

  • Singapore Zoo where wildlife adventures await amidst lush greenery and immersive habitats. Step into a world of wonders as you explore the diverse ecosystems and encounter fascinating animals from around the globe. With engaging exhibits, interactive experiences, and conservation efforts, the Singapore Zoo offers an enriching and unforgettable journey for visitors of all ages.

    • Hightlights

  • Best Time To Travel - March to September

      Things To Do - Embark on safari rides, feed gentle giants, and witness captivating animal shows

      Perfect Fit For - Animal Lovers, Families, Lil Ones, Nature Enthusiasts

    • Description

  • Singapore Flyer Asia's largest observation wheel offering breathtaking city views. Step into a capsule and soar above the cityscape, enjoying panoramic vistas of Marina Bay, Gardens by the Bay, and more. As an iconic landmark, it symbolizes Singapore's modernity and innovation, providing visitors with an unforgettable experience. Don't miss the opportunity to witness the city from a whole new perspective as you glide effortlessly through the sky

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - February to June

      Things To Do - Experience panoramic vistas, indulge in sky dining, and capture unforgettable memories

      Perfect Fit For - Thrill-seekers, Couples Seeking Romantic Experiences

    • Description

  • Sentosa Island is Singapore's premier island resort destination! Sentosa blends pristine beaches, top attractions, and lush greenery for the ultimate relaxation and adventure. From thrilling theme parks to serene nature trails, there's something for everyone to enjoy in this tropical paradise.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - December to March

      Things To Do - Enjoy thrilling rides at Universal Studios, relax on beaches, explore underwater wonders, and enjoy panoramic views from the Tiger Sky Tower

      Perfect Fit For - Theme Park Fans, Adventure Seekers, Families with Lil ones

    • Description

  • The only Universal Studios theme park in Southeast Asia, Step into the magic of Hollywood as you embark on thrilling rides, meet beloved characters, and immerse yourself in iconic movie-themed attractions. With seven themed zones and endless entertainment, Universal Studios Singapore promises an unforgettable adventure for visitors.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - February to May

      Things To Do - Experience heart-pounding rides, meet your favorite movie characters, and enjoy live entertainment

      Perfect Fit For - Fantasy Fans, Theme Park lovers, Families, Lil Ones

    • Description

  • River Safari Singapore stands as Asia's premier destination dedicated to river-themed wildlife exploration! Get ready to embark on an aquatic adventure as you explore the world's most iconic rivers and their fascinating inhabitants. From the mighty Amazon to the mysterious Mekong, there's so much to discover right here in the heart of Singapore.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - December to March

      Things To Do - Embark on river-themed boat rides, encounter giant pandas, visit freshwater exhibits, and enjoy educational shows

      Perfect Fit For - Families with Little ones, Nature Lovers

    • Description

  • Night Safari Singapore – the world's first nocturnal wildlife park! Get ready for an adventure unlike any other as you embark on a journey through the jungle after dark. Explore the natural habitats of over 2,500 nocturnal creatures representing 130 species. Hop on board the tram for a guided tour or explore walking trails for a closer look at fascinating creatures. It's an unforgettable wildlife experience under the stars!

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - November to March

      Things To Do - Embark on a tram ride, explore walking trails, witness nocturnal animals, and enjoy live shows

      Perfect Fit For - Thrill-seekers, Wildlife Enthusiasts

    • Description

  • S.E.A. Aquarium Singapore where the wonders of the ocean come to life! As one of the world's largest aquariums, get ready to explore a mesmerizing underwater world filled with over 100,000 marine creatures. From majestic sharks to vibrant coral reefs, there's something incredible waiting for you around every corner.

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - June to December

      Things To Do - Immerse yourself in underwater tunnels, witness captivating marine life, attend feeding sessions, and enjoy interactive exhibits

      Perfect Fit For - Marine Life Enthusiasts, Families, Little ones

    • Description

  • A world-class aviary in Mandai, replacing the iconic Jurong Bird Park, home to over 3,500 birds from 400 species, with immersive walk-in aviaries and interactive experiences


     

    • Hightlights

  •   Best Time To Travel - Year-round

    Things To Do - Walk through Lory Loft & Penguin Cove, experience bird feeding, and watch free-flight bird shows
